WHAT IS
GAZE ECONOMY?
// THE CONCEPT
The internet doesn't just transmit information — it processes it into a visual vocabulary. When a crisis, trend, or idea generates enough attention, it gets absorbed into meme culture: mocked, mourned, celebrated and recycled as GIFs. This is the gaze economy — the market of collective looking.

This visualisation asks: how thoroughly has a concept been codified into visual reflex? The denser the GIF library for a keyword, the more completely that idea has been processed by internet culture.
// THE CITY METAPHOR
Each column is a keyword — a building in the city. Its height reflects how many GIFs exist for that term (up to 15 visible, drawn from the top 30 results). Taller buildings = deeper cultural codification.

Buildings are grouped into districts by thematic cluster. The ground line is the present moment. Everything above it is what the internet has already absorbed. Scroll horizontally to move through the city.

Click any GIF to expand it and see its source context.
